About our college :


In 2018, a new government multi-disciplinary technical college has been started in Thiruvallur district's Sevvapet.This college is recognised by the DOTE and AICTE.

Location: Thiruvallur district, Sevvapet.

Type: Government multi-disciplinary technical college.

Established: In the year 2018.

Recognition: Accredited by the Directorate of Technical Education (DOTE).

Recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
